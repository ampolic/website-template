Regular property maintenance saves money and prevents bigger problems down the road.

## Spring

- Inspect irrigation systems for winter damage
- Clean gutters and downspouts
- Prune trees and shrubs before new growth
- Apply pre-emergent weed control

## Summer

- Maintain regular mowing schedule
- Water deeply and less frequently
- Monitor for pest and disease issues
- Trim back overgrown vegetation

## Fall

- Remove leaves regularly to prevent lawn damage
- Winterize irrigation systems before first freeze
- Apply slow-release fertilizer
- Schedule a final cleanup before snow
