Twice a year, every property needs a hard reset. Our seasonal cleanups clear out what the last season left behind and set the lawn and beds up for the one ahead.

## Spring Cleanup

- Leaf and debris removal from beds, lawns, and fence lines
- Pruning of shrubs and small ornamental trees
- Bed edging with crisp, defined lines
- Fresh mulch installation (cedar or hardwood, your choice)
- Pre-emergent weed treatment before soil temperatures rise
- First mow and full property blow-off

## Fall Cleanup

- Complete leaf removal and haul-away — multiple visits available
- Garden bed winterization and perennial cutbacks
- Final mow at reduced height to prevent snow mold
- Gutter and downspout debris clearing
- Winterizer fertilizer application to feed roots through the cold months

## Why It Matters

A mat of wet leaves left over winter smothers turf and breeds fungus; skipping spring pre-emergent means fighting crabgrass all summer. Cleanups are the cheapest insurance your landscape can buy.

## Timing

Spring cleanups begin in March and book out fast. Fall cleanups run October through early December. Recurring mowing customers get priority scheduling.
