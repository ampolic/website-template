Our seasonal cleanup service prepares your property for the changing weather.

## Spring Cleanup

- Leaf and debris removal from beds and lawns
- Pruning of shrubs and small trees
- Bed edging and fresh mulch installation
- Pre-emergent weed treatment
- Lawn aeration and overseeding

## Fall Cleanup

- Leaf removal and hauling
- Garden bed winterization
- Final mowing and trim
- Debris removal from gutters and downspouts
- Winter fertilizer application

## Timing

Spring cleanups begin in March. Fall cleanups run October through November. Schedule your appointment early as slots fill quickly.
