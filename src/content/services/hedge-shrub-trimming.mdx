Overgrown shrubs make even a well-mowed lawn look neglected. We trim for shape and for plant health — which means the right cut at the right time of year, not just a once-over with the hedge trimmer.

## What Is Included

- Shaping and sizing of hedges, foundation shrubs, and ornamentals
- Thinning cuts that open the canopy and encourage dense regrowth
- Deadwood removal from small ornamental trees
- Complete cleanup and haul-away of all trimmings
- Timing guidance so spring bloomers keep their flower buds

## Timing By Plant

Spring-flowering shrubs (lilac, forsythia, viburnum) get trimmed right after bloom. Summer bloomers and evergreens are shaped in late spring and again in late summer if needed. We track this so you don't have to.

## One-Time or Scheduled

Book a single visit to bring things back under control, or add two to three trimming visits per season to your maintenance plan and never think about it again.
