Nothing transforms a property faster than freshly edged beds and clean mulch. Beyond looks, a proper mulch layer holds soil moisture, buffers root temperature, and blocks the sunlight weeds need to germinate.

## What Is Included

- Redefined bed edges cut with a crisp, deep spade line
- Full bed weeding and removal of old, matted mulch where needed
- Two to three inches of premium cedar or dyed hardwood mulch
- Pre-emergent applied under the mulch for season-long weed suppression
- Careful clearance around trunks and stems — no mulch volcanoes

## Choosing a Mulch

- **Natural cedar** — light color, slow to break down, natural insect resistance
- **Dyed hardwood (brown or black)** — holds its color the full season, budget-friendly
- **Playground-certified** — for play areas and pet zones

## How Often

Most beds need a full refresh every one to two years, with a light top-dress in between. We measure your beds and quote by the yard, so you only pay for what your property actually needs.
