A great lawn is not an accident — it is the result of showing up every week and doing the small things right. Our mowing crews run the same route on the same day each week, so you always know when to expect us and your lawn never gets ahead of you.

## What Is Included

- Weekly mowing with commercial-grade equipment, blades sharpened daily
- Alternating mow patterns to prevent ruts and soil compaction
- Precision edging along walkways, driveways, and flower beds
- String trimming around trees, fences, mailboxes, and structures
- Full blow-off of all hard surfaces before we leave
- Height-of-cut adjusted through the season to protect the turf

## Why Weekly Matters

Mowing more than one-third of the blade at a time stresses the grass and invites weeds. A consistent weekly cut keeps the turf dense, shades out crabgrass, and produces clippings small enough to mulch back in as free fertilizer — no bagging, no mess.

## Who It Is For

Homeowners and small business owners who want consistent curb appeal without maintaining equipment or chasing a schedule. We service lawns from small city lots to multi-acre properties.
