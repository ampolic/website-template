Mowing keeps a lawn neat — feeding is what makes it thick and green. Our six-step program delivers the right nutrients at the right time and stays ahead of weeds instead of chasing them.

## The Six-Step Program

1. **Early spring** — balanced fertilizer plus crabgrass pre-emergent
2. **Late spring** — second pre-emergent pass and broadleaf weed control
3. **Early summer** — slow-release feeding with spot weed treatment
4. **Mid summer** — stress-formulated feeding with grub monitoring
5. **Early fall** — recovery fertilizer and broadleaf weed control
6. **Late fall** — winterizer application to build root reserves for spring

## What You Can Expect

- Noticeably thicker, darker turf by mid-season of year one
- Dandelions, clover, and crabgrass controlled before they spread
- Licensed applicators and clearly flagged treatments after every visit
- A written summary at every application telling you what we did and why

## Safe and Measured

Every product we apply is state-registered and put down at label rates by trained, licensed technicians. We will always tell you the re-entry window for kids and pets — usually just until the product dries.

## Pairing

This program pairs best with weekly mowing and a fall aeration and overseeding. Bundle all three and your lawn gets everything it needs in one plan.
