The sellers had two weeks before their home hit the market and a front yard that hadn't changed since the nineties. Their realtor's advice was simple: fix the first impression. We had one week on the schedule and used it.

## Scope of Work

- Removed overgrown yews blocking the front windows
- Designed and installed layered foundation plantings — boxwood, hydrangea, and ornamental grasses
- Re-cut all bed lines and installed black dyed hardwood mulch
- Double-cut and striped the lawn, edged every hard surface
- Power-washed the front walk and applied polymeric sand to the joints

## Results

The listing photos did the rest. The home went under contract the first weekend with multiple offers, and the realtor told us the buyers specifically mentioned the landscaping at the showing. Total project time: four days.
