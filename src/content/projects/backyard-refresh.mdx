This project involved a full backyard overhaul for a homeowner who wanted a low-maintenance outdoor space.

## Scope of Work

- Removed overgrown shrubs and debris
- Installed new hardscape edging along all beds
- Planted native drought-resistant perennials
- Applied fresh cedar mulch to all garden areas
- Restored and leveled the lawn area

## Results

The finished space is clean, maintainable, and ready for the family to enjoy throughout spring and summer. The new plantings will fill in over the next two growing seasons.
