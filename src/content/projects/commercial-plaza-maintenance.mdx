The property manager of this six-tenant retail plaza needed one contractor to own the grounds completely: mowing, beds, trimming, and cleanups, with invoices that made sense to an owners' association. The previous arrangement — three vendors and no clear responsibility — had left the entrances looking tired.

## Scope of Work

- Weekly mowing, edging, and hard-surface blow-off scheduled before business hours
- Monthly hedge and shrub maintenance across all storefronts
- Spring and fall cleanups with full leaf haul-away
- Seasonal planter rotations at both plaza entrances
- Single consolidated monthly invoice with per-visit reporting

## Results

Two years in, the plaza has had zero grounds-related tenant complaints — down from a running list under the previous vendors. The manager's words: "I stopped thinking about the landscaping, which is exactly what I'm paying for."
